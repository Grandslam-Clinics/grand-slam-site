Common Questions

Got Questions?
We've Got Answers.

What age groups do you coach?−
Players aged 8–18, from beginner through advanced. Every session is tailored to the individual's current skill level — no two plans are the same.
Where do sessions take place?+
We come to you. Sessions happen at local fields, gyms, or training facilities — wherever is most convenient for your family across the GTA.
How does team coaching work with existing staff?+
We collaborate with your existing coaches — our goal is to strengthen your program, not replace it. Packages are customized by roster size, skill levels, and frequency.
What makes Grand Slam different?+
Every Grand Slam coach is a former rep player or higher. We combine real playing experience with individualized training and a relentless focus on measurable, visible results.
What is your cancellation policy?+
Just give us 24 hours notice and we'll find a makeup time. We understand family schedules and are always flexible.
Is the free consultation really free?+
100%. Every new client gets a free, no-commitment consultation call. We talk through your player's goals and figure out the best plan — before anything is booked or charged.
